# Hyperlocal Deal Discovery Platform - Feature Expansion Roadmap

This document captures a broad roadmap of possible features that can be added to the Hyperlocal Deal Discovery Platform. It is designed to help with hackathon pitching, backlog planning, product strategy, and future development prioritization.

- Multi-store support for a single retailer account

- Store verification workflow for trusted retailers
- Support for multiple branches under one business

- Pause/resume deal publishing
- Bulk upload products using CSV or Excel
- Bulk edit pricing, discount, and quantity
- Duplicate existing product/deal entries
- Product inventory history and change log
- Deal version tracking
- Deal approval workflow for admins
- Product tags for richer filtering
- Support for product variants like size and flavor
- Barcode or SKU support
- Stock reservation for a short time window
- Auto-hide deals when stock reaches zero
- Auto-recommend discount percentages
- Auto-suggest expiry urgency labels
- Manual featured deal pinning for retailers
- Category-specific landing pages
- Search by product name, brand, or store
- Advanced filters with combined criteria
- Saved searches for customers
- Recently viewed deals
- Trending deals section
- Personalized featured section
- “Best value nearby” section
- “Expiring in 1 hour” section
- “Freshly added” deals section
- “Highest savings” section
- Dynamic homepage sections based on demand
